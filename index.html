DE LUCY RESTUARANT is a French restuarant which was located in Phnom Penh. We prepare and serve all kind of foods such as, bakery, breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We are also decorating events for our customers.

  • Deliver good quality and healthy food
  • Romantic and clean enviroment and view
  • fast service and friendly waiters/waitress.

At DE LUCY RESTUARANT, you will recieve the new feeling as if you were in France with your love one, with our fresh and romantic environment.

Why Choose De Lucy Restuarant?

De Lucy Restaurant serves a delicious foods with a good quality and healthy ingredients for our diners. We make yours dine more romantic and enjoyable with our indoor and outdoor views with a French decoration.
